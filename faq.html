Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers.

ADHYAAYA is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any college or university. Some events may have specific prerequisites, which will be mentioned on the event details page.

You can register for any event through our Registration Page. Simply fill out the form, select the events you wish to participate in, and complete the payment process if applicable. You will receive a confirmation email with your unique ID card.

Some of our flagship events and workshops have a nominal registration fee to cover costs. However, many other events, quizzes, and talks are completely free to attend. The fee for each event is listed on the registration page.

For most technical workshops, you will be required to bring your own laptop and charger. Any other specific software or hardware requirements will be communicated to registered participants via email before the workshop.

Yes, all registered participants who attend an event or workshop will receive a digital certificate of participation. Winners will receive separate certificates of achievement. You can download them from our Attendee Portal 5 days after the fest concludes.
