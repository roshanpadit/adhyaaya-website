Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register for events, such as your name, email address, and college name. This information is used solely for the purpose of event management and communication.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to process your registration, provide you with updates about the events, and generate participation certificates. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the information provided on our contact page.
